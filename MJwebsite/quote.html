About Michael Jackson

Full Name | Michael Joseph Jackson
Born | August 29, 1958
Died | June 25, 2009

Michael was an American singer, songwriter, and dancer, the one who is regarded as one of the most significant cultural figures of the 20th century and ond of the greatest entertainers.

He was pretty versatile, not only did he sing and dance well, but he also had a talent for songwriting, choreographing and even drawing, I had seen his sketches on the Internet, and they were amazing. I guess he was a genius in any possible aspect that you can imagine. Many of his songs are just like beautiful poems.

However, none of these are the main reason why I adored him so much. It was his warm and kind heart that I found attractive. He donated lots of money to the charities. He cared about the world and the homeless people so much that he wrote songs for them, like "the lost childer" and "Heal the world".

Even though many people misunderstood him and thought him as a freak, he still made his effort to help those in need. Many folks today still hate him after his death, but I believe a great amount of his fans around the world will love hime and respect him forever.

Rules

Use ↑ ↓ → ← to control the direction, press spacebar to eat food, carry the rock and shoot at monsters.
If monsters are hitted by the rock, they would faint, then you can press spacebar to collect them.
If you don't collect those fainted monsters, they would be active again soon.
You will lose when hitted a monster!
You will win when all monsters are collected!

The idea of this game is from Nitrome's Square Meal Game
